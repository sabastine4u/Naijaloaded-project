Wizkid ft Asake - Jogodo

Posted by  Jibade on February 1, 2026 10:49am

“Jogodo” by Wizkid ft Asake is a dazzling Afrobeats anthem released in January 2026 as the lead single from their joint EP tittled Real. Produced by Magicsticks the song features catchy Yoruba and Pidgin lyrics about celebration, nightlife and uninhibited fun, with Wizkid's smooth melodies and Asake's energetic flair blending into a vibrant, chart topping groove.
